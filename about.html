Our Mission

At Detache, we're revolutionizing how people navigate relationship endings and emotional recovery. We understand that breakups are among life's most challenging experiences, often leaving people feeling lost, overwhelmed, and unsure of how to move forward.

Our mission is to transform the healing process by combining evidence-based therapeutic approaches with cutting-edge AI technology. We provide tools that not only help you process emotions and gain insights but also actively support your journey toward emotional freedom and personal growth.

What Makes Us Different

Traditional approaches to breakup recovery often leave people waiting weeks for therapy appointments or relying on generic self-help resources. Detache bridges this gap by providing immediate, personalized support when you need it most.

🤖 AI-Powered Therapy

Our AI therapist is available 24/7, trained specifically in breakup recovery techniques and equipped with the latest therapeutic methodologies to provide compassionate, intelligent support.

📱 Instant Digital Detox

Our one-click photo removal tool uses advanced AI recognition to help you create a clean digital environment, removing triggers that can hinder your healing process.

📊 Data-Driven Insights

Track your emotional patterns, understand your healing journey, and receive personalized recommendations based on your unique recovery process.

🎧 Narrative Therapy Innovation

Listen to AI experts discuss your therapy sessions in podcast format, providing new perspectives and insights to accelerate your healing.
