Turn Your Breakup Into Your Breakthrough

Separation has the power to make or break your future. But it's not just about healing. Breakup gives you clarity, which is the driver behind everything you want to achieve. With the right tools, your pain becomes purpose, your rejection becomes direction, and your life transforms.

That's why at Detache, we don't just focus on getting over someone. We focus on ways you can harness separation energy and channel it into becoming the person you've always wanted to be, so you can reach your potential each day.

About Us

Detache is built with three pillars to help people navigate separations and realize their bold vision of themselves—their true potential. This can only be achieved with rigorous science-backed self work, and only pain and rejection pushes us to take that hard look at ourselves and go on this hero's journey. Human potential is one of a kind. We empower and reignite it during separations.
